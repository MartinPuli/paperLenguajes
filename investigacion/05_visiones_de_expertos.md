## Optimistas / disruptivos

### Dario Amodei (CEO, Anthropic)
- Venue: Council on Foreign Relations, "CEO Speaker Series", **10 de marzo de 2025** (moderó Mike Froman). Transcripción: https://www.cfr.org/event/ceo-speaker-series-dario-amodei-anthropic
- Cita (transcripción oficial CFR): *"…we'll be there in three to six months—where AI is writing 90 percent of the code. And then in twelve months, we may be in a world where AI is writing essentially all of the code. But the programmer still needs to specify… what is the overall app you're trying to make, what's the overall design decision?"*
- Matiz: es condicional ("may be"). El programador sigue especificando.

### Sam Altman (CEO, OpenAI)
- *Reflections* (blog, 6-ene-2025): *"We believe that, in 2025, we may see the first AI agents 'join the workforce'…"* https://blog.samaltman.com/reflections
- *The gentle singularity* (blog, 10-jun-2025): *"…writing computer code will never be the same."* · *"A lot more people will be able to create software… but experts will probably still be much better than novices, as long as they embrace the new tools."* https://blog.samaltman.com/the-gentle-singularity
- Stratechery (Thompson, 20-mar-2025): *"my basic assumption is that each software engineer will just do much, much more for a while. And then at some point, yeah, maybe we do need less software engineers."* · sobre % de código: *"in many companies, it's probably past 50% now."* https://stratechery.com/2025/an-interview-with-openai-ceo-sam-altman-about-building-a-consumer-tech-company/
